Bread

Bread is a staple food prepared from a dough of flour (usually wheat) and water, usually by baking.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Mix flour with water to create dough.
  2. Put the dough in a warm place with a moist towel above to help rise.
  3. Then magically have a cooked loaf of bread.
